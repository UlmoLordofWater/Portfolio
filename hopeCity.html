Hope City

Full Stack: Angular, Springboot, MySql, Netlify & Heroku

This project was our final for Bethel School of Technology. I worked with three other amazing men, Sean Rathbun, Jacob Hunt and Jeremy Gann. We decided to create an application that could help our cities and communities. Our plan was to have an event driven website, where people could come in and sign up for an account. They could say yes to events and attend them. Admins of the site would be hand picked and would be the ones to create events and help moderate the events. Currently we only have half of that implemented but we are continuing with the project and will be giving a finished product in the future.
